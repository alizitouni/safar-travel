Overview

A Journey Through Imperial Grandeur

Morocco is unlike anywhere else on earth — a place where ancient medinas pulse with life, where the Sahara stretches endlessly golden, and where Islamic architecture reaches its most breathtaking heights. This curated 7-night journey takes you through the imperial heartland: the vibrant chaos of Marrakech, the timeless spirituality of Fes, and the dreamlike blue streets of Chefchaouen.

Every element has been designed with the Muslim traveller in mind. Your riads are alcohol-free sanctuaries, your dining is certified halal, and your itinerary flows naturally around prayer times. Morocco is already one of the world's most Muslim-friendly destinations — we simply take it to another level.

Day by Day

Your Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Marrakech
Private VIP transfer from Marrakech Menara Airport to your luxury riad in the Medina. After settling in, an evening stroll through the legendary Jemaa el-Fnaa square as the city comes alive at sunset. Welcome dinner at a rooftop halal restaurant overlooking the Koutoubia Mosque.
Private transferRiad check-inWelcome dinner
Day 2
Marrakech — The Imperial City
Full day private guided tour of Marrakech. Visit the stunning Bahia Palace, the historic Saadian Tombs, the Ben Youssef Madrasa — one of Morocco's finest examples of Islamic architecture — and lose yourself in the labyrinthine souks. Afternoon at leisure in your riad's private courtyard.
Private guideBahia PalaceBen Youssef MadrasaSouk tour
Day 3
Atlas Mountains & Ait Benhaddou
Day excursion over the dramatic Tizi n'Tichka pass through the High Atlas Mountains to the UNESCO-listed Ait Benhaddou kasbah — one of Morocco's most iconic sights. Berber village lunch with traditional halal tagine, followed by a scenic drive back through the mountains.
Atlas MountainsUNESCO KasbahBerber lunch
Day 4–5
Sahara Desert Experience
Private 4x4 journey south to the Sahara. Arrive at the golden Erg Chebbi dunes in time for sunset camel trekking. Overnight at an exclusive luxury desert camp with private tents, traditional music, and a sky full of stars. Next morning, rise before dawn to watch the sunrise paint the dunes gold. Return journey via the dramatic Draa Valley oases.
Luxury campCamel trekSunrise dunesDraa Valley
Day 6
Fes — The Spiritual Heart
Fly or drive to Fes, Morocco's spiritual capital. Private guided tour of Fes el-Bali — the world's largest car-free urban area. Visit the famous tanneries, the Al-Qarawiyyin Mosque (the world's oldest university), and the ornate Bou Inania Madrasa. Evening at leisure to explore the ancient medina on your own terms.
Fes MedinaAl-QarawiyyinTanneriesPrivate guide
Day 7
Chefchaouen — The Blue Pearl
Morning drive to the magical blue city of Chefchaouen, nestled in the Rif Mountains. Wander the photogenic blue-washed streets at your own pace, shop for artisan crafts, and enjoy a long lunch at a terrace overlooking the medina. Afternoon at leisure before the drive to Casablanca for your departure flight.
Blue MedinaRif MountainsArtisan shopping
Day 8
Departure
Private transfer to Casablanca Mohammed V Airport or return to Marrakech. End of an unforgettable Moroccan journey.
Private transferDeparture assistance
