Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is all food on these packages halal-certified? ↓
Yes — every restaurant, hotel dining experience, and catered meal included in our packages is halal-certified or verified halal by our team on the ground. We do not work with any supplier that cannot provide halal assurance. In countries like Morocco, Turkey, and Malaysia this is straightforward as halal is the default. In Spain and Maldives we have specifically vetted every dining partner.
Are international flights included in the price? ↓
No — all package prices shown are "land only," meaning they cover accommodation, in-country transport, guides, activities, and meals as specified. International flights are not included because flight prices vary enormously depending on your departure city and dates. We can assist with flight recommendations and sometimes secure discounted rates through our airline partners.
Can packages be customised for our family's specific needs? ↓
Absolutely — in fact, customisation is at the heart of what we do. Every package can be adjusted for group size, travel dates, duration, accommodation category, dietary requirements beyond halal (allergies, preferences), wheelchair accessibility, children's ages, and any other specific need. Contact us and we will build an itinerary around your family specifically.
How do I pay and is it secure? ↓
We typically require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due 6–8 weeks before departure. We accept bank transfer, credit card, and other payment methods depending on your location. All payments are processed securely and receipts are provided. Our payment terms and cancellation policy are explained fully before any commitment is required.
What if I need to cancel or change my booking? ↓
We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is: full refund minus deposit if cancelled more than 60 days before departure; 50% refund between 30–60 days; no refund within 30 days (though we will always try to reschedule). We strongly recommend travel insurance, which we can help you obtain.
Are prayer facilities available throughout the journey? ↓
Yes — this is a non-negotiable part of every Safar Travel package. Every hotel is vetted for proximity to a mosque or prayer room. Prayer mats and Qibla direction are requested for every room. Itineraries are designed around prayer times, not against them. On group excursion days, prayer breaks are built into the schedule. We have never had a client miss a prayer on a Safar Travel package.
